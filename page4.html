Com2Tech Charity Workshop

Com2Tech is a Dunedin based charity. In the past they've worked with the Polytechnic BIT, doing workshops alongside past students. Hamish Smith approached me asking if I'd be able to manage it if it were to return. After asking around the students and gauging interest, I decided that I would take over the leadership role within the workshop. I am also entering the tech workforce next year, having charity work on my CV comes across as a big positive.

Com2Tech exists on two fronts, on one hand they run tutorial classes for families and the elderly on computer use, the other hand they are a charity organization getting second hand computer systems out to the less fortunate. I worked alongside Sarah and Tim, both of which being on the management team at Com2Tech. My role was to ensure that the machines that were to be given out were in proper working condition. 

The job took place weekly from 3PM to 5PM. I would come in early in order to set the room up for everyone. This involved arranging for monitors, keyboards and mice as well as supplying an air compressor, Windows 10 USB's and protective gear. At the workshop members would diagnose and repair computers before putting a fresh Window's 10 Home Edition instillation on, thus also clearing out all other data. These computers were then set up for a new user, cleaned out and packed up ready to go. After the workshop finished I would hang around to return keys, USB's and return the room to the way that I found it.

Health and safety was a concern of Com2Tech's. Their health and safety minimum guidelines state that to use an air compressor you must be using both ear muffs and glasses. After a period of investigation I was able to supply both of these.

We often will work alongside certification students who would be using the time period to work through their labs or their assignments. I would give them advice and assist with troubleshooting. Furthermore, the machines were a relatively safe environment for the students to practice within.

Next semester I will continue attending, however I will be needing a member of the project team to lead it. I intend to be present to assist with the transfer and in order to ensure everything goes smoothly.
